Ability Score:The numerical value of your ability after acounting for any negatives.

Ability Modifier:The bonus or penalty given by your ability scores, to actions requiring or useing them.

Armor Class (AC):A numerical Representation of avoidance in combat. Effected By Armor,size,dexterity and shields. This value must be met or exceeded to connect a blow in combat.

4E 3.5E 3E 2E

Attack of Opportunity:A single extra melee attack that can be made in a round, when a opponent within reach, takes a prevoking action. Cover prevents such an attack.
